About This Repository

Tobacco use remains the leading cause of preventable death worldwide. This repository provides healthcare professionals with access to evidence-based best practices, proven interventions, and comprehensive resources for tobacco prevention, cessation, and control.

Our collection includes programs focused on:

  • Tobacco use prevention and education
  • Cessation programs and interventions
  • Secondhand smoke exposure reduction
  • Policy implementation and advocacy
  • Clinical treatment protocols
